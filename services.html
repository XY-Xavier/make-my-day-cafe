Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for most events. For large corporate events or weddings, 4–6 weeks is ideal to ensure availability and proper planning.

What's included in the setup?

We bring everything — espresso machine, grinders, milk, syrups, cups, and a professional barista. We just need access to a power outlet and a flat surface.

Can you accommodate dietary needs?

Absolutely. We offer oat, almond, and soy milk alternatives, as well as decaf and caffeine-free options. Just let us know when booking.

Do you serve food as well?

We focus on coffee and beverages, but we can coordinate with local bakeries and caterers for pastries and light bites to complement your event.

How many guests can you serve?

We can serve events from 20 to 500+ guests. For larger events, we bring additional baristas and equipment to maintain service quality.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve the San Francisco Bay Area, including San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and surrounding cities. Contact us for locations outside this area.
